- Sucess rate is a business facing sucess metric, that measures sucess in terms of requests. A request is a loan that is triggered for a Blue Prism process to pick up, and if this request is completed within the established criteria - 3 retries without human intervention -, then it is considered a completion (Encompass sucess rate)
- Completion rate takes a business exceptions as a success since the path is handled accordingly.
